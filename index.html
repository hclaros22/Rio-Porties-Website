About Us

We love Portuguese Water Dogs for their friendly nature, intelligence, playful personalities, and the joy they bring to everyday life. They are wonderful with people, fun to be around, and can make a beautiful addition to a loving family.

Our Program

Our goal is to raise healthy, happy, loving companions for families who will cherish them for years to come. Our dogs are part of our family and are raised with attentive care and love.

Our puppies are raised in our home with their mom and dad. We focus on giving each puppy the love and care it needs with hands-on attention and a clean environment.

About Portuguese Water Dogs

Portuguese Water Dogs are intelligent, playful, loyal, and loving companions. They are known for their friendly personalities, athletic nature, and strong bond with their families.

This breed is active, people-oriented, and happiest when included as part of the family. With their affectionate temperament and energetic personalities, they can be a wonderful addition to the right home.

With proper training, some Portuguese Water Dogs may be able to assist people with:

  • autism
  • hearing loss
  • mobility disabilities
  • anxiety or emotional support needs
