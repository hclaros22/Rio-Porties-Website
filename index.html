Welcome

Welcome to Rio Portuguese Water Dogs. We raise happy, healthy, well-loved Portuguese Water Dogs in a loving family environment.

Who We Are

At Rio Portuguese Water Dogs, we love this breed for its playful personality, intelligence, and loving nature. Portuguese Water Dogs are friendly, fun, and wonderful companions for families. We are committed to raising puppies with care, attention, and love right in our home.

Our goal is to raise healthy, happy, loving companions for families who will cherish them for years to come. Our dogs are part of our family and are raised with attentive care every step of the way.

Our Program

Our puppies are raised in our home with their mom and dad in a family setting. We focus on giving each puppy a loving start with hands-on care and a clean, comfortable environment.

We believe in quality care, healthy development, and raising beautiful Portuguese Water Dogs that are ready to become a special part of your family.

About Portuguese Water Dogs

Portuguese Water Dogs are intelligent, playful, loyal, and loving companions. They are known for their friendly personalities, athletic nature, and strong bond with their families. With their fun energy and affectionate temperament, they can be a wonderful addition to the right home.

This breed is active, people-oriented, and happiest when included as part of the family. We love Portuguese Water Dogs for their big personalities, their sweet nature, and the joy they bring into everyday life.
