Welcome

Welcome to Rio Portuguese Water Dogs. We raise happy, healthy, well-loved Portuguese Water Dogs in a loving family environment.

About Us

We love Portuguese Water Dogs for their friendly nature, intelligence, playful personalities, and the joy they bring to everyday life. They are wonderful with people, fun to be around, and can make a beautiful addition to a loving family.

Our Program

Our goal is to raise healthy, happy, loving companions for families who will cherish them for years to come. Our dogs are part of our family and are raised with attentive care, love, and dedication every step of the way.

Our puppies are raised in our home with their mom and dad. We focus on giving each puppy the love and care it needs through hands-on attention and a clean, comfortable environment.

About Portuguese Water Dogs

Portuguese Water Dogs are intelligent, playful, loyal, and loving companions. They are known for their friendly personalities, athletic nature, and strong bond with their families. With their fun energy and affectionate temperament, they can be a wonderful addition to the right home.

This breed is active, people-oriented, and happiest when included as part of the family. We love Portuguese Water Dogs for their big personalities, sweet nature, and the joy they bring into everyday life.
